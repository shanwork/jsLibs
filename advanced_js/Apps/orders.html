  • On clicking the Start Orders button, Orders are given by the customer and ready to be processed
  • Processing takes place in the right (cyan background) colunn and progress of processing shows as % age completion
  • After processing, the order's pushed to the central (light blue) column, going to 'delivering' status (% age completion)
  • Once delivered, the order is moved to the delivered and complete list (left, pink column)
  • Orders get added (at the top of the stack) to the right column, until the 'looping duration' time span is reached
  • After the 'looping duration' is reached, existing orders run their course of processing and delivery. No new orders
