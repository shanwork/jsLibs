High level information of Shivpuran.
The Shiva Purana is one of eighteen Purana genre of Sanskrit texts in Hinduism, and part of the Shaivism literature corpus. It primarily centers around the Hindu god Shiva and goddess Parvati, but references and reveres all gods. The Purana has different versions, sizes and, in fact, 'divisions'(Samhitas) gvary somewhat .
